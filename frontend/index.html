About Me

Farzia Tabassum

Professional Bridal Henna Artist | Chennai

With over 7 years of professional expertise, I am proud to be recognized as one of Chennai's premier bridal henna artists. My work is a heartfelt fusion of tradition, artistry, and elegance‚Äîcrafted to reflect the individuality of each bride I have the honor of adorning.

I specialize in custom, intricate bridal mehndi using 100% organic, homemade henna, carefully prepared with natural ingredients to ensure deep, long-lasting stains and a safe, skin-friendly application. Every paste is crafted fresh, ensuring not only rich color but also a soothing and aromatic experience.

Whether it's a grand wedding celebration, an intimate engagement, or a festive occasion like Eid, Karva Chauth, or Baby Showers, my designs are personalized to match your style and significance of the moment. Artistic Boundaries & Respect

In honor of personal beliefs and cultural sensitivity, I do not offer facial portraits (such as dulha-dulhan faces or deity features) in my henna designs. My artistry focuses on beautiful patterns, motifs, and symbolic elements that celebrate tradition while respecting diverse beliefs.
